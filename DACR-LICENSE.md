**5.2 Diagnostic Question Architecture.** The three-question diagnostic framework in `questions/chris-diagnostic-questions.md` — including its structure of naming the mechanism, the loop closure target, and the counterfactual confidence test — constitutes proprietary intellectual property. Adapting this question architecture for other engagements without license is prohibited.

**5.3 Silent Distortion Window (SDW) Construct.** The SDW construct — specifically the architecture of a divergence between system-reported confidence and actual output accuracy, and the 30-to-90-day estimation methodology for drift detection lag — is an original construct of the Author. This construct may not be incorporated into any AI governance, MLOps, or data quality framework without Attribution and written license for commercial use.

**5.4 Chart Architecture.** The FRED-style diagnostic visualization system, including the dual-line confidence-vs-accuracy divergence chart (Chart 2), the multi-company trajectory chart (Chart 1), and the grouped readiness bar architecture (Chart 4), constitutes protected visual and analytical intellectual property. Reproducing these chart structures in consulting deliverables, investor presentations, or published research without Attribution and commercial license is prohibited.

**5.5 EBT Pathway 4 — Temporal Criteria Decay.** This specific pathway — the mechanism by which AI systems encode decision criteria at training time and fail to detect subsequent criteria invalidation — is an original extension of the Evaluative Bias Transference framework. It is distinct from general MLOps model drift concepts and is protected as an original named construct of the Author.
